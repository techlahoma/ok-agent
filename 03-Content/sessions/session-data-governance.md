## Abstract
Learn how to establish and maintain effective data governance practices in your organization. This session covers the fundamentals of data governance, including policies, procedures, and tools needed to ensure data quality, security, and compliance.

Topics covered include:
- Data governance frameworks and models
- Roles and responsibilities
- Data quality management
- Compliance and regulatory considerations
- Tools and technologies for data governance

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the importance of data governance
- Learn how to establish a data governance program
- Identify key roles and responsibilities
- Discover tools and techniques for implementation

## Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of data management concepts
- Experience working with organizational data
